<p><p>Vehicle refinish coating market consists of four main types: Primer, Base Coat, Top Coat, and Clear Coat. Primer is applied first to provide adhesion and corrosion protection. Base Coat is the colored paint that gives the vehicle its aesthetic appeal. Top Coat is applied to add gloss and protection. Clear Coat is the final layer that protects the finish from UV rays and scratches. Each type plays a crucial role in creating a durable and visually appealing finish on vehicles.</p></p>
