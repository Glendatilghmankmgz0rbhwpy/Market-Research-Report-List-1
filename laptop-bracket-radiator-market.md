<p><p>The Laptop Bracket Radiator market can be classified into three main types: Active, Passive, and Multi-Purpose. Active Laptop Bracket Radiators include built-in fans or cooling systems to actively dissipate heat from the laptop. Passive Laptop Bracket Radiators rely on natural convection or materials with high thermal conductivity to passively cool the laptop. Multi-Purpose Laptop Bracket Radiators offer a combination of active and passive cooling methods, providing more versatility and efficiency in managing laptop temperatures. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
