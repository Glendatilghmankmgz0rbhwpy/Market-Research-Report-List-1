<p><p>Fixed ANPR Systems Market includes various types of products such as ANPR cameras, ANPR software, frame grabbers, and triggers. ANPR cameras are specialized cameras designed to capture license plate images. ANPR software processes and analyzes these images to extract the license plate information. Frame grabbers are used to capture and convert analog video signals into digital format. Triggers are devices that activate the ANPR system, such as sensors that detect vehicles approaching a checkpoint. These products are essential components of fixed ANPR systems for accurate and efficient license plate recognition.</p></p>
