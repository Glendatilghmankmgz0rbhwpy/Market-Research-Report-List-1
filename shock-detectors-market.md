<p><strong>The Shock Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric</li><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Capacitors</li><li>Strain Gage</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shock detectors are sensors used to detect and measure mechanical shock or impact on a device or structure. The market types include piezoelectric sensors, which generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress; piezoresistive sensors, which change resistance under stress; capacitors, which store charge and discharge in response to shock; strain gage sensors, which measure deformation in response to shock; and other types such as MEMS (Micro Electro-Mechanical Systems) sensors. Each type of shock detector has its own unique applications and advantages.</p></p>
