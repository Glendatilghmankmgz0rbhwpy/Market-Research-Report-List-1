<p><ul><li>CMOS Chips</li><li>DMD Chips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Projection Display Chip market includes two main types: CMOS Chips and DMD Chips. CMOS Chips use complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology to produce images with high color accuracy and energy efficiency. On the other hand, DMD Chips, also known as digital micromirror devices, use tiny mirrors to reflect light and create images with high resolution and brightness. Both types of chips play a crucial role in the projection display industry, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
