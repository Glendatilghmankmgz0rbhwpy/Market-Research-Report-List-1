<p><p>Magnesium Hydroxide Paste is produced through two main methods: Chemical Synthesis Method and Physical Method. The Chemical Synthesis Method involves reacting magnesium oxide with water to form magnesium hydroxide. This method is commonly used in industrial settings due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, the Physical Method involves grinding magnesium oxide and water together to create a paste. This method is often preferred for small-scale production due to its simplicity and lower equipment requirements.</p></p>
