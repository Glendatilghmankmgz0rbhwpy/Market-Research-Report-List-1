Tubular sleeves provide basic protection, braided sleeves offer flexibility and expansion capabilities, corrugated sleeves are durable and provide abrasion resistance, spiral sleeves are easy to install and remove, and other variations cater to specific needs such as heat resistance or electromagnetic interference shielding.
