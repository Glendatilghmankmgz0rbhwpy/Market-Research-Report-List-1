<p><p>The PPH pipe and fittings market includes two main types of products - PPH pipes and PPH fittings. PPH pipes are thermoplastic pipes made from polypropylene homopolymer, known for their high resistance to chemicals and temperature fluctuations. PPH fittings, on the other hand, are components used to connect PPH pipes, such as elbows, tees, and couplings. Both PPH pipes and fittings are widely used in industries like chemical processing, water treatment, and wastewater management due to their durable and corrosion-resistant properties.</p></p>
