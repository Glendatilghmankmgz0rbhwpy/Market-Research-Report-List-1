<p><p>Drain cleaner and disinfectant products are available in three main types - gel, liquid, and powder. Gel cleaners are thick and stick to the walls of the drain, providing a longer contact time for effective cleaning. Liquid cleaners are easy to pour and can quickly penetrate and break down clogs. Powder cleaners are convenient for measuring out precise amounts and are often used for deeper cleaning tasks. Each type has its own strengths and is suitable for different drain-cleaning needs.</p></p>
