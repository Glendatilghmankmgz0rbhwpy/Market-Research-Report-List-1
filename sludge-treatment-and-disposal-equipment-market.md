<p><p>Sludge treatment and disposal equipment market includes various types of equipment used for managing sludge generated in industrial and municipal wastewater treatment processes. Some common types of equipment are screw press, centrifuges, belt filter press, and others. Screw press equipment works by applying pressure to dewater the sludge. Centrifuges use centrifugal force to separate solids from the sludge. Belt filter press utilizes a series of belts to compress and dewater the sludge. Other types of equipment may include sludge dryers, incinerators, or anaerobic digesters, depending on the specific needs of the sludge treatment and disposal process.</p></p>
