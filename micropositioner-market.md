<p><p>Micropositioners are categorized based on their axis of movement, including single stage, double stage, and triple stage micropositioners. They are also classified by product type, such as manual or electric micropositioners. Single stage micropositioners move along one axis, while double stage micropositioners move along two axes, and triple stage micropositioners move along three axes. Manual micropositioners are operated by hand, while electric micropositioners are powered by electricity for precise positioning in various industries such as electronics, semiconductor, and medical devices.</p></p>
