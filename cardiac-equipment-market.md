<p><p>The cardiac equipment market consists of various types of devices used in the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions. Balloon pumps help in increasing blood flow to the heart, while cardiopulmonary bypass pumps support heart function during surgeries. Cardiac ultrasound devices enable non-invasive imaging of the heart. CRT-P and CRT-D are specialized pacemakers that resynchronize heartbeats, with the latter also having defibrillation capabilities. Defibrillators are used to restore normal heart rhythm, pacemakers regulate heart rate, and ventricular assist devices help pump blood in cases of heart failure.</p></p>
