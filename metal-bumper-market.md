<p><p>Metal bumper market can be classified into two main types: front bumper and rear bumper. Front bumpers are designed to protect the front end of vehicles from collisions and impacts. They help absorb and distribute the force of the impact to minimize damage to the vehicle and its occupants. Rear bumpers serve a similar purpose for the rear end of vehicles. Both front and rear bumpers are typically made of durable metals such as steel or aluminum, providing essential protection for vehicles on the road.</p></p>
