<p><p>Liquid Crystal on Silicon Based Spatial Light Modulators are categorized into two main types: Reflective Spatial Light Modulators and Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators. Reflective Spatial Light Modulators utilize a reflective surface to manipulate light waves, making them ideal for applications requiring high resolution and contrast ratios. Each type offers unique advantages for different optical manipulation needs.</p></p>
