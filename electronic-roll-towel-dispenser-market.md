<p><p>The electronic roll towel dispenser market consists of two primary types: recessed mounting and wall mounting. Recessed mounting refers to the installation of the dispenser inside a wall cavity, making it flush with the surface. This provides a sleek and unobtrusive appearance. On the other hand, wall mounting involves fixing the dispenser directly onto the wall's surface. This type is more practical in spaces where recessed mounting is not an option. Both options offer convenience and touchless operation, providing efficient and hygienic hand drying solutions.</p></p>
