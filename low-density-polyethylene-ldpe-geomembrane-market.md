<p><p>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) Geomembrane market types include Single Layer Geomembranes, Double Layer Geomembranes, and Three Layer Geomembranes. Single Layer Geomembranes consist of one layer of LDPE, providing basic containment functions. Double Layer Geomembranes have an additional protective layer for enhanced durability and strength. Three Layer Geomembranes offer even greater protection with an extra layer designed for specific applications such as chemical resistance or UV protection. These variations cater to different project requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
