<p><p>Metal casing market is segregated into different types such as stamping, die casting, extrusion, and CNC machining. Stamping involves shaping metal sheets using a stamping press. Die casting uses molds to produce complex metal casings with high precision. Extrusion involves forcing metal through a die to create long, constant cross-section shapes. CNC machining uses computer-controlled tools to remove material from a metal block and create intricate casings. Each market type offers unique advantages and is used based on specific requirements of the end product.</p></p>
