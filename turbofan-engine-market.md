<p><strong>The Turbofan Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-bypass Turbofan</li><li>Afterburning Turbofan</li><li>High-bypass Turbofan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low-bypass Turbofan engines have a larger exhaust area compared to their intake area, making them efficient at high speeds. Afterburning Turbofan engines have an additional combustion chamber after the turbine to produce extra thrust during takeoff or combat situations. High-bypass Turbofan engines have a large fan at the front that bypasses most of the incoming air around the engine core, making them fuel-efficient and quieter, ideal for commercial aircraft. These different types cater to specific needs and requirements in the aviation industry.</p></p>
