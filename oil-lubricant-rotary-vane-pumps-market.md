<p><p>Oil lubricant rotary vane pumps are categorized into single-stage and two-stage types based on their design and functionality. Single-stage oil lubricant rotary vane pumps have one rotating vane that generates suction and discharge of the fluid. On the other hand, two-stage oil lubricant rotary vane pumps have two rotating vanes for enhanced performance and efficiency. Single-stage pumps are suitable for low to medium pressure applications, while two-stage pumps are ideal for high-pressure applications that require increased pumping efficiency.</p></p>
