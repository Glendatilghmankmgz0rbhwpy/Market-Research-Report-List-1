<p><strong>The FPGA Evaluation Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SRAM</li><li>FLASH</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FPGA evaluation board market can be segmented into SRAM and FLASH based on the type of memory used in the boards. SRAM based boards offer faster access times and can be reprogrammed quickly, making them suitable for rapid prototyping and testing. On the other hand, FLASH based boards offer non-volatile memory, allowing for the retention of program data even when power is turned off. This makes them ideal for applications requiring long-term storage of configuration data and firmware. Both types cater to different requirements and preferences of FPGA developers.</p></p>
