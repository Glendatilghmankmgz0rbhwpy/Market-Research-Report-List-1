<p><p>Commercial treadmills come in two main types: manual treadmills and motorised treadmills. Manual treadmills require the user to power the belt themselves through walking or running, providing a more challenging workout. On the other hand, motorised treadmills have a motor that powers the belt, allowing for varying speeds and inclines at the touch of a button. Motorised treadmills are often preferred in commercial settings for their ease of use and versatility, while manual treadmills are better suited for those looking for a more intense workout.</p></p>
