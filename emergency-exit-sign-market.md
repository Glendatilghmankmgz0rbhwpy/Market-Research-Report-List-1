<p><p>Emergency exit signs come in two main types: Electrical exit signs and Non-electrical exit signs. Electrical exit signs are powered by electricity and typically use LED lights for illumination. They are more common in commercial buildings and require regular maintenance to ensure proper functioning. Non-electrical exit signs, on the other hand, do not rely on electricity and are often made of glow-in-the-dark materials or have reflective surfaces to remain visible in the event of a power outage. These signs are commonly used in residential buildings or places where electricity may not be readily available.</p></p>
