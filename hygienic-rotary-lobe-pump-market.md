<p><ul><li>Self-priming</li><li>Conditioned Self-priming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygienic rotary lobe pumps come in two main market types: self-priming and conditioned self-priming. </p><p>Self-priming pumps are capable of automatically drawing fluid into the pump upon startup without the need for manual priming. This feature makes them ideal for applications where the pump may run dry or where manual priming is difficult.</p><p>Conditioned self-priming pumps are a variation of self-priming pumps that are specifically designed for pumping high-viscosity fluids or liquids with entrained air. They are equipped with additional features to improve priming capabilities in these challenging applications.</p></p>
