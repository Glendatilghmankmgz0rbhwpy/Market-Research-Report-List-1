<p><ul><li>Interior Fiberglass Mesh</li><li>External Fiberglass Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass meshes are used in both interior and external applications in the construction industry. Interior fiberglass mesh is typically used for drywall joint taping, crack repair, and wall reinforcement. It provides a strong foundation for plaster and paint. External fiberglass mesh is used for external wall insulation systems, stucco reinforcement, and roof waterproofing. It helps prevent cracks and improves the durability of the structure. Both types of fiberglass meshes offer high strength, flexibility, and resistance to weather and chemicals.</p></p>
