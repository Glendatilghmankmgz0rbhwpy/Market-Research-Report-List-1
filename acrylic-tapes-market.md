<p><p>Acrylic tapes market is divided into two main types: double sided and single sided. Double sided acrylic tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing them to stick to two surfaces at once. These tapes are commonly used for bonding materials together. On the other hand, single sided acrylic tapes have adhesive only on one side, making them ideal for securing items to a surface. Both types of acrylic tapes are popular choices for various applications due to their strong bonding properties and versatility.</p></p>
