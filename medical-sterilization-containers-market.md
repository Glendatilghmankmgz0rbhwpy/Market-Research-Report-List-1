<p><p>Medical sterilization containers come in two main types - filter type and valve type. Filter type containers contain specialized filtration systems to prevent contamination and ensure sterilization. These filters help remove particles and bacteria during the sterilization process, maintaining a sterile environment for medical instruments. On the other hand, valve type containers utilize sealing valves to maintain a sterile environment without the need for filters. Both types of containers are essential for maintaining the sterility of medical instruments in healthcare settings.</p></p>
