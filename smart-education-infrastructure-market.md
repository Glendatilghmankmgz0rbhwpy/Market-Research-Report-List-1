<p><p>The Smart Education Infrastructure market consists of three main types: Hardware (IaaS), SaaS, and PaaS. IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service) involves providing hardware resources over the internet, allowing educational institutions to access and manage servers, storage, and networking. SaaS (Software as a Service) delivers software applications over the internet, enabling schools to use educational tools without the need for installation or maintenance. PaaS (Platform as a Service) offers a platform for schools to develop, run, and manage applications without the complexity of building and maintaining the infrastructure.</p></p>
