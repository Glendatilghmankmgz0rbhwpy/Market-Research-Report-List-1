<p><strong>The Pavement Grooving Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pavement grooving machines are primarily categorized into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I machines are designed for grooving new concrete pavements, while Type II machines are used for restoring existing pavements. Type I machines are equipped with diamond blades to create precise and clean grooves in fresh concrete, whereas Type II machines use milling cutters to remove deteriorated pavement material and create new grooves. Both types of machines play a crucial role in maintaining and enhancing the safety and longevity of road surfaces.</p></p>
