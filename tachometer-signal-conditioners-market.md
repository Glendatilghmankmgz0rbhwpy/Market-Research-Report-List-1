<p><p>Tachometer signal conditioners are used to convert signals from tachometers into a format that can be easily measured and analyzed. There are two types of market categories based on input voltage - low voltage input and high voltage input. Low voltage input conditioners are designed for applications where the input voltage is relatively low, typically under 24 volts. High voltage input conditioners, on the other hand, are used for applications where the input voltage is higher, such as in industrial machinery or automotive engines. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate signal processing and analysis.</p></p>
