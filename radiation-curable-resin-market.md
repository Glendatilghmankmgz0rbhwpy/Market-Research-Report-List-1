<p><strong>The Radiation Curable Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EB Curable Resins</li><li>UV Curable Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation curable resins are divided into two main types - EB curable resins and UV curable resins. EB curable resins are cured using electron beams, which penetrate deep into the resin to initiate the curing process. On the other hand, UV curable resins use ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process on the surface of the resin. Both types of radiation curable resins offer fast curing times, high durability, and excellent environmental friendliness, making them ideal for a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
