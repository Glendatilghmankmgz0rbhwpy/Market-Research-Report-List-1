<p><p>Binders for batteries are materials used to hold the active materials, such as anode and cathode, together in a battery. Anode binders, specifically designed for anode materials, improve the adhesion and stability of active materials, enhancing battery performance and lifespan. On the other hand, cathode binders enhance the bonding between active materials in the cathode, resulting in better performance and increased energy storage capacity. Both types of binders play crucial roles in ensuring the efficiency and durability of batteries in various applications.</p></p>
