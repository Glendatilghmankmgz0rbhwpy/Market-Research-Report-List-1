<p><p>Automotive Power Distribution System market consists of two main types: Hardwired Vehicle Power Distribution System and Configurable Vehicle Power Distribution System. Hardwired systems are fixed in their wiring and connections, making them less flexible but more reliable. On the other hand, Configurable systems allow for customization and reconfiguration according to specific needs and requirements, providing more flexibility at the expense of potentially greater complexity. Both types play crucial roles in managing the distribution of power in vehicles efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
