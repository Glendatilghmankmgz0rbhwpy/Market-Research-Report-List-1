<p><p>Orthopedics diagnostic devices market includes X-ray systems, CT scans, and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) machines. X-ray systems use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of bones and joints, while CT scans provide detailed cross-sectional images of the body. MRI machines use magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images of bones, joints, and soft tissues. These diagnostic devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring orthopedic conditions such as fractures, arthritis, and sports injuries.</p></p>
