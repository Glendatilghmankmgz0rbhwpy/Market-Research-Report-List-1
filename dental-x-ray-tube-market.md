<p><p>The Dental X-ray Tube Market is divided into two main types based on the type of anode: Stationary Anode and Rotating Anode. Stationary anode tubes are simpler in design and are used for lower power applications. Rotating anode tubes, on the other hand, are more advanced and are used for higher power applications where continuous X-ray production is required. Rotating anode tubes have the advantage of dissipating heat more efficiently, allowing for longer periods of use without overheating.</p></p>
