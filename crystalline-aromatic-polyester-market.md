<p><p>Crystalline Aromatic Polyester can be divided into two main types based on their structure: Single Crystal and Multi Crystal. Single Crystal Crystalline Aromatic Polyester contains a regular and uniform arrangement of molecules, resulting in a highly ordered structure. This type tends to have higher strength and stiffness properties. On the other hand, Multi Crystal Crystalline Aromatic Polyester consists of multiple crystal structures with varying orientations, leading to a more random arrangement of molecules and different mechanical properties compared to Single Crystal.</p></p>
