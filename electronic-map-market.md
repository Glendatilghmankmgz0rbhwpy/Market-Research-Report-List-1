GIS is used for capturing, storing, analyzing, and managing spatial data. LiDAR technology uses laser pulses to measure distances to create three-dimensional models. Digital orthophotography combines aerial photography with digital mapping techniques. Aerial photography uses images taken from aircraft to create maps. The "others" category may include technologies such as satellite imagery or mobile mapping.</p></p>
