<p><p>NdFeB Bonded Magnets are available in two main types: Injection Molding and Pressing. In the Injection Molding market, magnets are made by injecting a molten mixture of NdFeB powder and a polymer binder into a mold. This process allows for complex shapes and high precision components to be created. In the Pressing market, magnets are manufactured by pressing a mixture of NdFeB powder and binder into a mold under high pressure. This method is ideal for creating simple shapes quickly and cost-effectively.</p></p>
