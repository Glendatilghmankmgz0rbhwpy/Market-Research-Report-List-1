<p><strong>The Mold Discharging Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Release Agent</li><li>External Release Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal release agents are materials added directly to the mold cavity to prevent sticking of the molded part to the mold surface. They provide a thin film on the mold cavity surface to facilitate easy release of the molded part. External release agents, on the other hand, are applied to the outer surface of the mold to aid in the release of the molded part from the mold. Both types of mold discharging agents play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient mold release processes in various industries.</p></p>
