<p><p>Gallium Nitride (GaN) opto semiconductor devices consist of three main market types: Opto Device, Power Device, and RF Device markets. Opto devices utilize GaN for applications such as LEDs and laser diodes for lighting and display purposes. Power devices are used in power electronics for high-frequency switching applications due to GaN's high electron mobility. RF devices make use of GaN for high power amplifiers and transistors in wireless communication systems. Each market type serves a specific purpose in leveraging the benefits of GaN technology.</p></p>
