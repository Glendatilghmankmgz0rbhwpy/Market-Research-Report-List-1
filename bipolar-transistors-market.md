<p><p>Bipolar transistors come in two main types, PNP and NPN. PNP transistors have a layer of N-doped semiconductor between two layers of P-doped semiconductor, while NPN transistors have a layer of P-doped semiconductor between two layers of N-doped semiconductor. Both types are commonly used in electronics for amplification and switching applications. PNP transistors are known for their high current capability, while NPN transistors are known for their fast switching speeds. The choice between PNP and NPN transistors depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
