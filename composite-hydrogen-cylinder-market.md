On the other hand, Type IV cylinders are fully composed of composite materials, offering enhanced safety and higher energy storage capacity for high-pressure applications. Both types are increasingly being adopted in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and energy due to their superior performance and sustainability benefits.</p></p>
