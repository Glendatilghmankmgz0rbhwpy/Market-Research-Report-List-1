<p><p>High Resolution Spectrometers are advanced analytical instruments used for determining the chemical composition of substances. There are two main types of spectrometers - Molecular spectrometers and Atomic spectrometers. Molecular spectrometers analyze the molecular structure of compounds by measuring the absorption, emission, or scattering of electromagnetic radiation. Atomic spectrometers, on the other hand, analyze the elemental composition of substances by measuring the interaction of atoms with electromagnetic radiation. Both types of spectrometers are essential tools in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental testing.</p></p>
