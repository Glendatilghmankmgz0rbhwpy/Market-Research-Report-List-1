<p><p>Real-time PCR (RT-PCR) Fluorescence Probe market includes various types such as Taqman, Molecular Beacons, and Minorgroove Binder Oli-godeoxynucleotide Conjugate (MGB-ODN). Taqman probes are widely used for their high specificity and sensitivity in detecting target sequences. Molecular Beacons are hairpin-shaped probes that emit fluorescence when they bind to their target sequence. MGB-ODN probes contain minor groove binders that enhance their stability and specificity. Each type of probe offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and reliability in real-time PCR applications.</p></p>
