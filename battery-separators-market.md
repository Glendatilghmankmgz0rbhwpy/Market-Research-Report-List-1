<p><p>Battery separators market includes three main types: nonwoven fibers, polymer films, and ceramic. Nonwoven fibers are made from materials like polyester and polyethylene, providing high porosity for efficient electrolyte flow. Polymer films are thin, flexible layers made from materials like polyethylene and polypropylene, offering good electrical insulation. Ceramic separators are rigid and provide high thermal stability and mechanical strength. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications within the battery industry to enhance overall performance and safety.</p></p>
