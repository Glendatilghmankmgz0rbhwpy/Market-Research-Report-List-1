<p><p>Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs) are devices used for emergency situations to signal for help and provide precise location information to search and rescue teams. The market is segmented into 406MHz Locator Beacons and 121.5MHz Locator Beacons. 406MHz beacons are more reliable and accurate, as they are monitored by satellite systems globally. On the other hand, 121.5MHz beacons are less reliable and only detectable by aircraft. Both types are essential tools for outdoor enthusiasts, aviators, and maritime workers in distress situations.</p></p>
