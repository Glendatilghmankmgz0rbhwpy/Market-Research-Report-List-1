<p><p>Bariatric medical chairs come in two main types: manual adjustment and automatic adjustment. Manual adjustment chairs require users to manually adjust the chair's height, backrest, and armrests, providing more control over positioning. On the other hand, automatic adjustment chairs use motorized mechanisms to adjust these features with the push of a button, offering convenience and ease of use for individuals with mobility challenges. Both types of chairs cater to the specific needs of bariatric patients, providing comfort and support during medical procedures.</p></p>
