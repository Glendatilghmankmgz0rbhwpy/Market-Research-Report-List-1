<p><p>Corrective maintenance in the municipal tree maintenance services market refers to the repair or replacement of trees that are already damaged or posing a risk to public safety. This type of maintenance is reactive in nature, responding to issues as they arise. Preventive maintenance, on the other hand, involves regular inspections and care to prevent potential issues before they become problems. It focuses on maintaining the health and structural integrity of trees to prolong their lifespan and minimize the need for corrective action.</p></p>
