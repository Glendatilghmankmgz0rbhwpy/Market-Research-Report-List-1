<p><p>Nickel-based superalloys are categorized into types based on their alloying elements. Ni-Cu superalloys contain nickel and copper, providing high strength and corrosion resistance. Ni-Cr superalloys combine nickel and chromium, offering excellent high-temperature strength and oxidation resistance. Ni-Mo superalloys contain nickel and molybdenum, giving them exceptional toughness and creep resistance. Other types of nickel-based superalloys may include additional elements like cobalt or tungsten for specific properties such as improved weldability or thermal stability.</p></p>
