The Aluminum Method involves reacting aluminum with ferric chloride solution, while the Active Aluminum Hydroxide Method utilizes active aluminum hydroxide as the raw material. The Aluminum Oxide Method involves transforming aluminum oxide into PAFC, and the Aluminum Chloride Method uses aluminum chloride to produce the compound.
