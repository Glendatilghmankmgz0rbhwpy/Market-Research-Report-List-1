<p><p>PCM paint stands for Phase Change Material paint, which is a type of paint that incorporates phase change materials (PCMs) into their formulation. PCMs are substances that absorb and release thermal energy during the process of changing from solid to liquid and vice versa. When used in paint, PCM can help regulate indoor temperatures by absorbing excess heat during the day and releasing it at night, creating a more comfortable indoor environment.</p><p>The PCM paint market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing demand for energy-efficient and sustainable building materials.
