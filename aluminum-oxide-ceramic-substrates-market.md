<p><p>Aluminum oxide ceramic substrates are available in three main types based on their alumina content: 95% alumina, 97% alumina, and 99% alumina. The 95% alumina substrates are cost-effective and suitable for general purpose applications. The 97% alumina substrates offer higher purity and strength, making them ideal for demanding thermal and electrical insulating applications. The 99% alumina substrates provide the highest level of purity, strength, and thermal conductivity, suitable for high-performance electronic and semiconductor applications.</p></p>
