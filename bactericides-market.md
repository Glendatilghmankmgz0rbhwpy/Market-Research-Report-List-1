<p><p>Bactericides market types include active chlorine, iodine, concentrated alcohols, phenolic substances, and cationic surfactants. Concentrated alcohols, like ethanol and isopropyl alcohol, are commonly used as disinfectants. Phenolic substances have strong antimicrobial properties.
