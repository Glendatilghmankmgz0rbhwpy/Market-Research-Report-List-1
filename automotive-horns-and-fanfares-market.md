<p><p>Automotive horns and fanfares can be categorized into three main types - air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns are typically louder and produce a deeper sound by using compressed air. Electronic horns are operated by an electric current and are commonly found in modern vehicles. Electromagnetic horns use a coil of wire to produce a sound and are often used in emergency vehicles. Each type of horn has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the vehicle and its intended use.</p></p>
