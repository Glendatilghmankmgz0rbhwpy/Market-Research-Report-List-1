<p><strong>The Touch Type Nitrogen Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touch type nitrogen analyzers are commonly divided into two main types: colorimetric method and electrode method. </p><p>Colorimetric method nitrogen analyzers operate by measuring the color change in a sample when it reacts with a specific reagent, providing a quantitative measurement of nitrogen content. </p><p>On the other hand, electrode method nitrogen analyzers work by measuring the electrical conductivity changes in a sample as nitrogen is converted into ammonia, offering a reliable and accurate analysis of nitrogen levels. Both methods have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries for nitrogen analysis.</p></p>
