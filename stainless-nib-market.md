<p><p>Stainless nib markets are categorized into three main types based on their tip sizes: F (fine) nibs, M (medium) nibs, and B (broad) nibs. F nibs produce thin, precise lines suitable for detailed writing or drawing tasks. M nibs provide a balance between line thickness and smoothness, making them a versatile choice for everyday use. B nibs create bold, thick lines that are ideal for calligraphy, signatures, or creating striking visual effects in artwork. Each nib type caters to different preferences and purposes.</p></p>
