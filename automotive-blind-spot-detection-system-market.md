<p><p>The automotive blind spot detection system market consists of various types of sensors, including radar sensor, ultrasonic sensor, LIDAR sensor, and others. Radar sensors use radio waves to detect objects in the vehicle's blind spot, while ultrasonic sensors use sound waves for the same purpose. LIDAR sensors use laser beams to create a 3D map of the vehicle's surroundings. Other types of sensors may include cameras or infrared sensors. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications in blind spot detection systems.</p></p>
