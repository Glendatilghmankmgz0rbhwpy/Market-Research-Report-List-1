<p><p>The Vehicle Horn Market consists of three main types: Air Horns, Electronic Horns, and Electromagnetic Horns. Air Horns use compressed air to produce a loud noise, often found in commercial vehicles. Electronic Horns use electrical components to create sound and are common in modern vehicles. Electromagnetic Horns utilize electromagnetism to generate a loud noise and are often used in motorcycles. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications, catering to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
