<p><strong>The Disposable Syringes Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP-H</li><li>PP-R</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable syringes can be made from various types of polypropylene, including PP-H and PP-R. PP-H refers to high heat resistant polypropylene, which is suitable for applications where the syringe may be exposed to high temperatures. PP-R refers to random copolymer polypropylene, which is known for its high chemical resistance and impact strength. These different types of polypropylene cater to the specific needs of the disposable syringe market, providing options for varying levels of heat resistance and durability.</p></p>
