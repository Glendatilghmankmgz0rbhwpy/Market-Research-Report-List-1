<p><p>PA Hot Melt Adhesive market types include Powder and Granule forms. Powder adhesives are finely ground particles that are melted and applied as a liquid, creating a strong bond when cooled. Granule adhesives consist of small pellets that are melted into a liquid form for application. Both types offer advantages such as quick setting times, strong bonds, and versatility in application methods. They are commonly used in industries such as packaging, woodworking, and automotive for their adhesive properties.</p></p>
