<p><p>Fire Fighting Chemicals market is divided into four main types: Dry Chemicals, Wet Chemicals, Dry Powder, and Foam-based. Dry Chemicals are commonly used in Class A, B, and C fires, while Wet Chemicals are specifically designed for Class K fires. Dry Powder is effective against Class D fires involving combustible metals, and Foam-based chemicals are ideal for Class A and B fires, creating a blanket that suppresses vapors and prevents reignition. Each type of chemical plays a critical role in extinguishing different types of fires.</p></p>
