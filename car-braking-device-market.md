<p><strong>The Car Braking Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Box System</li><li>One-Box System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-Box System and One-Box System are two types of car braking devices that are commonly used in the market. The Two-Box System includes a separate master cylinder and a separate brake booster, providing improved safety and performance. On the other hand, the One-Box System combines the master cylinder and brake booster into a single unit, offering a more compact design and easier installation. Both systems have their own advantages and are popular choices among car manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
