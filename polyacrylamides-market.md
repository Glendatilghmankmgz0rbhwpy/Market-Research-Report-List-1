<p><p>Polyacrylamides are classified into different types based on their ionic properties. Non-ionic polyacrylamide (PAMN) is used for industrial wastewater treatment and paper making. Anionic polyacrylamide (APAM) is commonly used for water treatment in municipal and industrial sectors. Cationic polyacrylamide (CPAM) is utilized in wastewater treatment and sludge dewatering. Other types of polyacrylamides include amphoteric polyacrylamide and hydrophilic polyacrylamide, each serving specific purposes in various industries such as agriculture, mining, and petroleum.</p></p>
