<p><strong>The Smart Pet Collar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS Based</li><li>Radio Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart pet collars come in two main types: GPS-based and radio-based. GPS-based collars use global positioning technology to track the location of the pet in real time, allowing owners to easily locate their furry friends if they go missing. On the other hand, radio-based collars use radio frequencies to establish a communication link between the collar and a receiver, providing a more localized tracking option. Both types of collars offer effective ways for pet owners to keep track of their pets and ensure their safety.</p></p>
