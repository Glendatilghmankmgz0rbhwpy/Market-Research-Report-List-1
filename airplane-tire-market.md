<p><strong>The Airplane Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Ply</li><li>Radial Ply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bias Ply and Radial Ply are two types of airplane tires that differ in their construction and material composition. Bias Ply tires have layers of fabric cords crisscrossed at angles under the tread, providing a sturdy and durable tire suitable for heavy load applications. On the other hand, Radial Ply tires have fabric cords laid at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, offering improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and a longer lifespan. Both types cater to different needs in the airplane tire market, with Bias Ply tires commonly used for commercial airliners and Radial Ply tires preferred for military aircraft.</p></p>
