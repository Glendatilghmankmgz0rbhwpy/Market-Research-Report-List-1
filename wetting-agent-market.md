<p><p>Wetting agents are used to improve the spreading and sticking properties of liquids on surfaces. Liquid wetting agents are in liquid form and are typically applied through spraying. They are effective for treating large areas. On the other hand, granular wetting agents come in the form of solid granules and are commonly used for spot treatments or in areas where liquids cannot be easily applied. Both liquid and granular wetting agents serve the same purpose of enhancing wetting properties, but they differ in their application methods and suitable uses.</p></p>
