<p><p>Micro-Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) market consists of different types based on the design of the machine. Open MRI machines have open sides and are suitable for claustrophobic patients. Closed MRI machines have a tunnel-like design and provide higher resolution images. Standard Bore MRI machines are common in hospitals and diagnostic centers. Wide-Bore MRI machines have a larger opening which accommodates larger patients and reduces feelings of claustrophobia. Each type of MRI machine caters to different patient needs and preferences in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
