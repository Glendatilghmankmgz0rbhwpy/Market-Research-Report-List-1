<p><p>Containerboard market consists of two major types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard is the flat facing that makes up the outer layers of corrugated board, providing strength and rigidity to the packaging. Corrugating medium is the fluted or ridged layer that goes between the linerboards, providing cushioning and support. Both linerboard and corrugating medium are essential components in the production of corrugated boxes and packaging materials, serving different functions but working together to create durable and versatile packaging solutions.</p></p>
