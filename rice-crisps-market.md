<p><p>Rice crisps have two main market types based on the type of rice used: glutinous rice base and non-glutinous rice base. Glutinous rice base crisps are made from sticky rice, giving them a chewy texture and a slightly sweet flavor. These are popular in Asian markets for their unique taste and texture. Non-glutinous rice base crisps are made from regular rice varieties and have a lighter, crispier texture. They are more commonly found in Western markets and are often flavored with savory seasonings. Both types offer a delicious and crunchy snack option for consumers worldwide.</p></p>
