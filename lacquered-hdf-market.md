<p><ul><li>Single-Faced</li><li>Double-Faced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lacquered HDF is available in two main market types: single-faced and double-faced. Single-faced lacquered HDF has a finish applied only to one side of the board, making it suitable for applications where only one side will be visible. Double-faced lacquered HDF, on the other hand, has a finish applied to both sides of the board, providing a more consistent appearance and making it ideal for applications where both sides will be visible. Both types offer durability, moisture resistance, and aesthetic appeal for various interior design projects.</p></p>
