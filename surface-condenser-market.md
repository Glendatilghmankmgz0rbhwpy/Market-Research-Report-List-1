<p><strong>The Surface Condenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Flow Surface Condenser</li><li>Regenerative Surface Condenser</li><li>Evaporative Surface Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface condensers are used in power plants to convert steam back into water for reuse in the boiler. There are three main types of surface condensers: Central Flow Surface Condenser, which uses a central cooling water tube; Regenerative Surface Condenser, which preheats the feedwater using exhaust steam; and Evaporative Surface Condenser, which uses air as the cooling medium. Each type has its own advantages and applications, but all serve the common purpose of increasing the efficiency of the power generation process.</p></p>
