<p><ul><li>Rubber Fenders</li><li>Foam Fenders</li><li>HALO Fenders</li><li>Pneumatic Fenders</li><li>Tug Fenders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine fenders are essential in protecting marine vessels and docks from damage during berthing. Rubber fenders are the most commonly used type due to their durability and ability to absorb impact. Foam fenders are lightweight and offer good energy absorption. HALO fenders provide protection against hull damage and promote smooth berthing. Pneumatic fenders are versatile and can be easily transported and installed. Tug fenders are specifically designed for tugboats to assist in maneuvering larger vessels. Each type of fender serves a specific purpose in the marine industry.</p></p>
