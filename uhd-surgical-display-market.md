<p><p>UHD surgical displays are available in two main types: LED UHD displays and LCD UHD displays. LED UHD displays utilize light-emitting diodes to produce images with high brightness and contrast, making them ideal for surgical environments where clarity is essential. On the other hand, LCD UHD displays use liquid crystal technology to achieve sharp and detailed images, offering a more cost-effective option for healthcare facilities. Both types of displays are designed to meet the demanding requirements of surgical procedures while providing superior image quality.</p></p>
