<p><p>Silicon tetrachloride is available in two main market types: electronics grade and technical grade. Electronics grade silicon tetrachloride is a high purity form used in the production of semiconductor chips and other electronic components. It undergoes stringent purification processes to remove impurities and ensure consistency in performance. On the other hand, technical grade silicon tetrachloride is a lower purity form used in industrial applications such as the production of silicones, fumed silica, and other chemicals. It is less expensive than electronics grade silicon tetrachloride but may contain impurities that are acceptable for non-electronic uses.</p></p>
