<p><p>Roll-core transformers are classified into two main market types: Dry Type and Oil-immersed Type. Dry Type transformers are filled with resin or epoxy instead of oil, making them more environmentally friendly and suitable for indoor applications where the risk of oil leakage is a concern. Oil-immersed Type transformers, on the other hand, are filled with mineral oil for effective cooling and insulation. They are commonly used in outdoor or high-power applications where higher efficiency and reliability are required.</p></p>
