<p><p>The Vehicle Proportioning Valve Market consists of three types of valves: Master Cylinder-Mounted Proportioning Valves, which are located near the master cylinder and regulate the pressure of the front and rear brakes; Load Sensing Proportioning Valves, which adjust brake force based on the weight distribution of the vehicle; and Combination Valve-Mounted Proportioning Valves, which combine proportioning valve function with other features like metering and pressure differential valves. Each type of valve plays a crucial role in ensuring proper braking performance and vehicle safety.</p></p>
