TypeA gaskets are designed with a raised face flange, providing superior sealing capabilities. TypeB gaskets have a flat face flange, suitable for applications requiring low bolt loads. TypeC gaskets are designed for raised face flanges with an added outer ring for extra protection and stability. Each type caters to different sealing requirements and specifications, offering versatility and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
