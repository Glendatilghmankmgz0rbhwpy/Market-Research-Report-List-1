<p><p>Stereo Bluetooth headsets are divided into two main market types: on-ear headsets and over-ear headsets. On-ear headsets rest directly on the ears, providing a lightweight and compact design suitable for mobility. They offer decent sound quality at an affordable price but may be less comfortable for extended wear. Over-ear headsets enclose the entire ear, delivering superior sound quality and noise isolation. They are bulkier and more expensive, but provide a more immersive audio experience and greater comfort for longer periods of use.</p></p>
