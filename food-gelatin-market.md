<p><p>Food gelatin is divided into three main types: skin gelatin, bone gelatin, and halal gelatin. Skin gelatin is derived from the collagen found in the skin of animals, while bone gelatin is sourced from the bones and connective tissues. Halal gelatin is made in accordance with Islamic dietary laws. Each type of gelatin serves different purposes and is used in a variety of food products such as gummy candies, marshmallows, and gelatin desserts. These gelatins play a significant role in the food industry due to their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
