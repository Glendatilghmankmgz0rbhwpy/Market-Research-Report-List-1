<p><strong>The Automotive Air Distribution System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Air Duct</li><li>Twinsheet Thermoformed Air Duct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional air duct system in automotive air distribution involves the use of rigid ducts made from materials like metal or plastic to direct air flow throughout the vehicle cabin. On the other hand, twinsheet thermoformed air ducts are made using a process where two plastic sheets are heated, shaped, and then sealed together, providing a lightweight and cost-effective alternative to conventional ducts. Both types of air distribution systems play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and effective heating, ventilation, and air conditioning in vehicles.</p></p>
