<p><p>Ultra HD Set Top Box (STB) market includes various types such as IPTV, Satellite, DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television), OTT (Over-The-Top), and Hybrid market. IPTV STBs deliver television content through internet protocol networks. Satellite STBs receive signals from satellites to provide TV content. DTT STBs receive digital signals through terrestrial antennas. OTT STBs stream content directly over the internet. Hybrid STBs combine traditional broadcast services with internet-based content, offering a seamless viewing experience for consumers. Each market type caters to different preferences and technological capabilities.</p></p>
