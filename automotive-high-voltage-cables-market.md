<p><p>Automotive high voltage cables market is divided into two main types: shielded cables and unshielded cables. Shielded cables are designed with an additional layer of protection to reduce electromagnetic interference and maintain signal integrity. They are ideal for applications where reliability and durability are crucial. On the other hand, unshielded cables are more cost-effective and simpler in design, but may be more susceptible to interference. Both types play a vital role in powering the electronics and systems in modern vehicles.</p></p>
