<p><ul><li>Public Clouds</li><li>Private Clouds</li><li>Hybrid Clouds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Public Clouds offer shared resources over the internet. Private Clouds offer dedicated resources for a single organization. Hybrid Clouds combine features of both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to use a combination of on-premises, private cloud, and public cloud services. Public Clouds are cost-effective but offer less control. Private Clouds provide more security and customization but are more expensive. Hybrid Clouds provide flexibility to meet varying business needs, ensuring a balance between cost, security, and control.</p></p>
