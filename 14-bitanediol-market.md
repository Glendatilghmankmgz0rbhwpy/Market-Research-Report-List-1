<p><p>1,4 Butanediol is commonly produced using different processes such as Reppe process, Davy process, Butadiene process, and Propylene oxide process. The Reppe process involves the reaction of acetylene and formaldehyde, while the Davy process uses maleic anhydride as a starting material. The Butadiene process utilizes butadiene as a raw material, and the Propylene oxide process involves the reaction of propylene oxide with formaldehyde. Each process has its own advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the market.</p></p>
