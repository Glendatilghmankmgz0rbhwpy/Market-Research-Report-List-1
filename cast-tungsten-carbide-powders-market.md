<p><p>Cast tungsten carbide powders are classified based on grain sizes, with three main categories: Grain Sizes <1 µm, Grain Sizes 1-10 µm, and Grain Sizes >10 µm. Powders with grain sizes less than 1 µm are known for their high hardness and wear resistance. Powders with grain sizes between 1-10 µm offer a balance of hardness and toughness. Powders with grain sizes larger than 10 µm are preferred for applications requiring impact resistance and thermal conductivity. Each type has unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
