<p><p>Student RFID Tracking Systems consist of three main markets: Tags, Readers, and Middleware. Tags are the physical devices attached to student IDs or belongings for tracking. Readers are the devices that read the information from the tags and collect data. Middleware is the software that processes and manages the data collected from the readers. Each market plays a crucial role in the overall system, with tags providing the physical tracking mechanism, readers collecting the data, and middleware organizing and analyzing the information.</p></p>
