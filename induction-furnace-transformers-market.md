<p><p>Oil-filled transformers are filled with mineral oil, which acts as a coolant and insulating medium. These transformers are typically used in heavy-duty industrial applications due to their high efficiency and ability to withstand high temperature and moisture levels. On the other hand, dry type transformers utilize epoxy resin or cast resin as an insulating material, making them more suitable for indoor or enclosed environments where oil-filled transformers are not practical. Dry type transformers are also more environmentally friendly as they do not require oil for cooling.</p></p>
