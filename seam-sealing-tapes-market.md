<p><p>Seam sealing tapes come in two main types: single-layered and multi-layered. Single-layered tapes consist of a single layer of adhesive coated onto a backing material, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for waterproofing seams. On the other hand, multi-layered tapes have multiple layers of adhesive and backing materials, offering enhanced durability and performance in harsh conditions. Both types of tapes are widely used in various industries such as outdoor apparel, tents, and military gear to prevent water penetration through seams.</p></p>
