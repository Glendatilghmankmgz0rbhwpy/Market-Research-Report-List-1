<p><p>A truck engine brake, also known as a compression brake or Jake brake, is a braking system used in large trucks to assist in slowing down the vehicle by using the engine's power. This not only helps improve braking efficiency but also reduces wear and tear on the wheel brakes, prolonging their lifespan.</p><p>The Truck Engine Brake Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period. <p><p>Truck engine brake market includes compression release brake and exhaust brake types. Compression release brake operates by limiting the flow of air into the cylinders, causing the engine to slow down. On the other hand, an exhaust brake works by restricting the flow of exhaust gases, creating back pressure which helps in slowing down the truck. Both types of brakes are commonly used in heavy-duty trucks to provide additional braking power and improve overall vehicle safety during descents and on slippery roads.</p></p>
