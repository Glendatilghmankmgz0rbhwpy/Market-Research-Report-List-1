<p><p>PET/CT scanners are used for molecular imaging in diagnosing and monitoring various diseases. There are two main types in the market: Digital PET/CT and Analog PET/CT. Digital PET/CT scanners use digital technology for better image quality, faster scan times, and lower radiation doses. Analog PET/CT scanners use traditional analog technology with slower scan times and higher radiation doses. Digital PET/CT scanners are preferred for their advanced imaging capabilities, whereas Analog PET/CT scanners are still used in certain settings for their reliability and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
