<p><p>Dental prosthesis cleaners market can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I cleaners refer to products specifically designed for cleaning removable dental prostheses like dentures, denture adhesive, and denture brushes. On the other hand, Type II cleaners are meant for cleaning fixed prostheses like crowns, bridges, and implants. Type II cleaners are usually more advanced and effective in removing plaque and bacteria buildup on fixed prostheses, whereas Type I cleaners are tailored for daily cleaning and maintenance of removable prostheses.</p></p>
