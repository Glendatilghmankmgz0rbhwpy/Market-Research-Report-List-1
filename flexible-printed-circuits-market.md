<p><p>The flexible printed circuits market is divided into three main types: Single Panel, Double Panel, and Multilayer Panel. Single Panel circuits consist of a single layer of conductive material, suitable for simple electronic devices. Double Panel circuits have two layers of conductive material, allowing for more complex circuit designs. Multilayer Panel circuits have multiple layers of conductive material, providing even greater design flexibility and functionality for advanced electronic devices. Each type of circuit offers different levels of complexity and functionality to cater to various electronic needs.</p></p>
