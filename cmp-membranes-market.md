<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMP Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>5-zone, 6-zone and 7-zone</li><li>Below 5-zone (3-zone, etc.)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP membranes are categorized based on the number of zones they contain, which determines the level of precision in wafer planarization. 5-zone membranes offer moderate polishing uniformity, while 6-zone membranes provide increased control over material removal. 7-zone membranes offer the highest level of precision for achieving ultra-smooth surfaces. Below 5-zone membranes such as 3-zone offer lower precision but are suitable for less demanding applications. Each type of membrane caters to different requirements in the semiconductor industry, offering varying levels of performance and cost.</p></p>
