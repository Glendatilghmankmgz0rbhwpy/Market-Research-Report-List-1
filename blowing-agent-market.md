<p><p>Blowing agents are chemical compounds used in the production of foams and insulation materials. There are several types of blowing agents in the market, including HCFCs, HFCs, HCs, and others. HCFCs are ozone-depleting chemicals being phased out due to their harmful environmental impact. HFCs are non-ozone depleting but have a high global warming potential. HCs are natural compounds with low global warming potential. Other types of blowing agents may include hydrocarbons or water-based compounds, offering environmentally friendly alternatives for foam production.</p></p>
