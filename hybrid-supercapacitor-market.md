<p><p>The Hybrid Supercapacitor market is categorized into Double Layer Type, Pseudocapacitive Type, and Others. Double Layer Type supercapacitors store energy through the separation of charge at the interface between electrolyte and electrode. Pseudocapacitive Type supercapacitors store energy through fast and reversible faradaic reactions at the electrode surface. Other types of supercapacitors include asymmetric and symmetric supercapacitors, which offer unique characteristics and applications. Overall, these different types cater to a variety of energy storage needs in the market.</p></p>
