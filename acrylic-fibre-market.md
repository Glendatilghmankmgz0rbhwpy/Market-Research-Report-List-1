<p><p>Acrylic fiber market can be classified into three main types: staple fiber, tows, and tops. Staple fiber refers to short fibers used in textiles and non-woven applications. Tows are continuous bundles of filaments typically used in textiles, upholstery, and technical applications. Tops are combed and parallelized fibers used for spinning yarn. Each type of acrylic fiber serves a specific purpose in various industries, providing versatility and durability in products ranging from clothing to industrial materials.</p></p>
