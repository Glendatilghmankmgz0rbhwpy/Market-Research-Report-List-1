<p><p>Lab scale tablet presses are commonly classified into two types: single-punch and rotary presses. Single-punch presses are ideal for small-scale production and testing due to their simplicity and ease of use. On the other hand, rotary presses are more advanced and efficient, capable of producing a higher volume of tablets in a shorter amount of time. Rotary presses are commonly used in pharmaceutical and research laboratories where larger quantities of tablets are needed for various studies and experiments.</p></p>
