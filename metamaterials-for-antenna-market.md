<p><p>Metamaterials for Antenna Market includes various types such as Negative-index Metamaterials (NIM), Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials, Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMMs), and Others. NIM have simultaneously negative permittivity and permeability, enabling unique properties like reverse electromagnetic wave propagation. SNG metamaterials have only one negative characteristic, such as negative permittivity. HMMs exhibit hyperbolic dispersion, leading to improved performance in certain applications. The "Others" category encompasses a variety of metamaterials with distinct properties and applications in antenna technology.</p></p>
