<p><p>Microfluidic flow control systems include various components such as chips, pumps, syringes, sensors, connectors, and others. Chips are used for controlling the flow of fluids on a microscale. Pumps are used to generate flow within the system. Syringes are often used for precise fluid manipulation. Sensors are utilized for monitoring and controlling the flow characteristics. Connectors are used to ensure proper connections within the system. Other components may include valves, filters, and mixers for additional functionality in microfluidic systems.</p></p>
