<p><p>Adhesives for Automotive Cameras and LiDAR market includes two main types: dual cure adhesives and single cure adhesives. Dual cure adhesives provide a two-step curing process, offering both UV light and moisture curing capabilities for strong bond strength. On the other hand, single cure adhesives only require one curing method, typically either UV light or moisture. Both types of adhesives are essential in securing automotive cameras and LiDAR systems in place, ensuring reliable performance and durability in vehicles.</p></p>
