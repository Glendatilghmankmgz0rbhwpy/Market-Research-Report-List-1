<p><p>Lathe machines are classified into two main types: vertical lathes and horizontal lathes. Vertical lathes have a vertical spindle axis, allowing for large, heavy workpieces to be processed. They are often used for operations such as turning, drilling, and milling. Horizontal lathes, on the other hand, have a horizontal spindle axis, making them suitable for handling smaller to medium-sized workpieces. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the manufacturing industry, providing precision and efficiency in metalworking processes.</p></p>
