<p><p>Gel filled fiber cables are used in both single-mode and multi-mode markets. Single-mode cables have a smaller core size, allowing for the transmission of data over longer distances with higher bandwidth and less signal loss. They are typically used in long-distance applications such as telecommunications and data centers. On the other hand, multi-mode cables have a larger core size and are designed for shorter distances with lower bandwidth requirements, making them suitable for LAN networks and shorter data transmissions.</p></p>
