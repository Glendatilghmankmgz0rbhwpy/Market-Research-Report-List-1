<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microlens arrays are used in various applications such as imaging, light manipulation, and beam shaping. Aspherical microlens arrays have non-uniform shapes which help in correcting aberrations and focusing light more efficiently. On the other hand, spherical microlens arrays have uniform curvature which simplifies their fabrication process and makes them cost-effective. Both types of microlens arrays are commonly used in cameras, projectors, sensors, and optical communication systems to improve resolution, enhance contrast, and reduce distortion.</p></p>
