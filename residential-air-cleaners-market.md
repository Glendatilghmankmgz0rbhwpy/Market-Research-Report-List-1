<p><p>Residential air cleaners are divided into various types based on their filtration technology. HEPA filters are highly efficient at capturing particles, while active carbon filters are effective at removing odors. Electrostatic precipitators use an electrical charge to attract particles, ion and ozone generators release ions or ozone to purify the air. Other types of air cleaners may utilize different technologies or combinations of the above methods to improve indoor air quality. Each type offers unique benefits and features to cater to different household needs.</p></p>
