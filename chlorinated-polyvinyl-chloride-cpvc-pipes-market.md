<p><strong>The Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride (CPVC) Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion PVC</li><li>Injection PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride (CPVC) Pipes Market includes two main types - Extrusion PVC and Injection PVC. Extrusion PVC involves heating and melting the PVC material before pushing it through a mold to create the desired shape. This method is commonly used for producing pipes and fittings. On the other hand, Injection PVC involves injecting molten PVC into a mold under high pressure to create complex shapes or small components. This method is often used for producing connectors, valves, and other intricate parts for CPVC pipes.</p></p>
