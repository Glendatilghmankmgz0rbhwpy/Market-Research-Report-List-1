<p><p>Valve Regulated Lead Acid Batteries are available in two main types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel. AGM batteries use a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, making them spill-proof and maintenance-free. They are commonly used in vehicles and UPS systems. Gel batteries have silica added to the electrolyte, forming a gel-like substance that reduces the risk of leakage and allows for mounting in various positions. They are often used in applications that require deep cycling capabilities, such as solar energy storage.</p></p>
