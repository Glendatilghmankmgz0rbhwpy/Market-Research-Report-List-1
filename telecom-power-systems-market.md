<p><p>Telecom power systems are classified into two main types: Indoor and Outdoor. Indoor telecom power systems are designed to be installed within a building or shelter, providing power for telecom equipment such as servers and switches. On the other hand, Outdoor telecom power systems are ruggedized units that can withstand harsh environmental conditions, providing power for equipment located outside, such as cell towers. Both types of systems are crucial for maintaining reliable communication networks and ensuring uninterrupted service for telecom companies.</p></p>
