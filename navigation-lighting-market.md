<p><p>Navigation lighting is categorized based on the intensity of light they emit. Low light intensity navigation lights are suitable for smaller vessels or calm waters where visibility is not a primary concern. Medium light intensity navigation lights are for mid-sized vessels operating in moderate visibility conditions. High light intensity navigation lights are designed for larger vessels or areas with poor visibility such as during bad weather or night-time navigation. Each market type caters to specific vessel sizes and visibility requirements to ensure safe navigation in different conditions.</p></p>
