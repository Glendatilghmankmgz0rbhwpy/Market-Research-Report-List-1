<p><p>Electrolytic weld cleaning fluids are used in the post-weld cleaning process to remove oxidation, scale, and flux residues from surfaces. There are two main types of electrolytic weld cleaning fluids: water-based cleaning fluids and solvent-based cleaning fluids. Water-based cleaning fluids are environmentally friendly and easy to use, making them a popular choice for many applications. Solvent-based cleaning fluids are effective for removing tough residues but may pose health and environmental risks due to their chemical composition. Both types of fluids have their own advantages and limitations in the market.</p></p>
