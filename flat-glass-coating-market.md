<p><p>Flat glass coatings are typically categorized into four main types: polyurethane, epoxy, acrylic, and others. Polyurethane coatings offer high durability and scratch resistance, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Epoxy coatings provide strong adhesion and chemical resistance, suitable for industrial applications. Acrylic coatings are known for their weather resistance and UV protection, commonly used in outdoor settings. The "others" category includes specialized coatings such as ceramic or silicone-based options, offering unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
