<p><p>Switching Mode Power Supply market includes two main types: AC/DC and DC/DC. AC/DC power supplies convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC), making them suitable for powering electronic devices that require a stable DC output. On the other hand, DC/DC power supplies convert one DC voltage level to another, providing voltage regulation and isolation. Both types of power supplies are widely used in various applications such as consumer electronics, industrial equipment, automotive electronics, and telecommunications.</p></p>
