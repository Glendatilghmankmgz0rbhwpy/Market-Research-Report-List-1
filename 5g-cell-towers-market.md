<p><p>5G Cell Towers Market Types represent different categories of cell towers based on their size and coverage area. Macro towers are large and cover broad geographical areas, making them ideal for dense urban areas. Small cells are smaller in size and provide coverage for specific locations within a macro network. Pico cells have even smaller coverage areas, suitable for indoor spaces like offices or shopping malls. Femto cells are the smallest and are typically used for residential or small business coverage. Each type serves a specific purpose in expanding and enhancing 5G network coverage.</p></p>
