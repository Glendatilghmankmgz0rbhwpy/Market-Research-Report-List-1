<p><p>Acoustic beamforming arrays market can be classified into two main types: Two-Dimensional (2D) and Three-Dimensional (3D) arrays. Two-dimensional arrays are typically linear or planar in shape, providing beamforming in two dimensions. On the other hand, three-dimensional arrays extend beamforming capabilities into a third dimension, allowing for more precise sound localization and shaping. Both types offer unique advantages and applications in areas such as surveillance, communication, and noise cancellation. The choice between 2D and 3D arrays depends on specific requirements and desired performance.</p></p>
