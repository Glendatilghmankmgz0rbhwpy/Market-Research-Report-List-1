<p><p>Perfluoroalkoxy (PFA) coatings market is divided into two main types based on the polymerization method used: aqueous medium polymerization method and nonaqueous medium polymerization method. Aqueous medium polymerization method involves the use of water as the medium for polymerization, while nonaqueous medium polymerization method uses non-water based solvents. These two methods offer different properties and advantages, such as improved adhesion and durability for aqueous medium polymerization method, and better resistance to chemicals and heat for nonaqueous medium polymerization method.</p></p>
