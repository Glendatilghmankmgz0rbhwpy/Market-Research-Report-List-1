<p><strong>The Photonic Bandgap Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Core</li><li>Solid Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photonic Bandgap Fiber market encompasses two main types - Hollow Core and Solid Core fibers. Hollow Core fibers have a low refractive index core surrounded by a photonic bandgap structure, allowing light to propagate through air or a low-index gas. These fibers are ideal for applications requiring low nonlinearity and dispersion. On the other hand, Solid Core fibers have a solid core with a photonic bandgap cladding, offering high light confinement and guiding properties. They are more suitable for applications that require high power handling and high nonlinear effects.</p></p>
