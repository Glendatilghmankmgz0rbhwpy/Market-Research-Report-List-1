<p><p>Loading dock shelters are commonly classified into three types: mechanical, inflatable, and others. Mechanical dock shelters are made of rigid materials such as steel or aluminum and provide a sturdy enclosure around the loading dock. Inflatable dock shelters, on the other hand, use air chambers to create a flexible seal around the opening of the dock. Other types of dock shelters may include retractable or sliding shelters that can be adjusted based on the size of the incoming vehicle. Each type of dock shelter offers unique benefits and is suited for different loading dock environments.</p></p>
