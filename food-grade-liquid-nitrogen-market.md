<p><p>Food Grade Liquid Nitrogen Market Types include Cryogenic Distillation and Pressure Swing Adsorption. Cryogenic Distillation involves separating nitrogen from air by cooling it to extremely low temperatures, while Pressure Swing Adsorption separates nitrogen by using adsorbent materials to selectively capture the gas under high pressure. Both methods are used to produce food-grade liquid nitrogen, which is commonly used in food preservation, packaging, and processing due to its ability to quickly freeze and maintain the quality of food products.</p></p>
