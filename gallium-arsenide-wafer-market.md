<p><strong>The Gallium Arsenide Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SC GaAs</li><li>SI GaAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) wafers are commonly classified into two categories based on the conductivity type of the material: SC GaAs (semi-conductive GaAs) and SI GaAs (semi-insulating GaAs). SC GaAs wafers are used in various electronic devices where conductivity is required, such as high-frequency applications. SI GaAs wafers, on the other hand, are preferred for devices that require high resistance and low conductivity, like in radar systems and microwave circuits. Both types of GaAs wafers serve different purposes in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
