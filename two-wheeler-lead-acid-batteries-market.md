<p><strong>The Two Wheeler Lead Acid Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VRLA Batteries</li><li>Flooded Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two Wheeler Lead Acid Batteries Market has two main types: VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) Batteries and Flooded Batteries. VRLA Batteries are sealed, maintenance-free batteries that do not require regular water topping up and are ideal for applications where minimal maintenance is desired. On the other hand, Flooded Batteries require regular topping up with distilled water to maintain electrolyte levels and are more common in traditional lead acid battery applications. Both types provide reliable power for two-wheeler vehicles but differ in maintenance requirements.</p></p>
