<p><p>Miniature cables are available in various types based on their size and specifications, with common options being 32 AWG, 34 AWG, and 36 AWG. The term "AWG" stands for American Wire Gauge, which is a standard measurement system for the diameter of wire. The lower the AWG number, the thicker the wire. 32 AWG cables are thicker and capable of carrying higher currents, while 36 AWG cables are thinner and used for applications requiring flexibility and space-saving. Each type serves different purposes in various industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
