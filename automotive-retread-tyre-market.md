<p><p>Automotive retread tire market consists of two main types: Pre Cure and Mold Cure. Pre Cure retreading involves applying a new tread to a tire casing before it is cured, while Mold Cure retreading involves applying the tread to the casing and then curing them together in a mold. Both methods help extend the life of tires by replacing worn-out treads, making them a cost-effective and environmentally-friendly option for commercial vehicles. These market types cater to different needs and preferences of customers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
