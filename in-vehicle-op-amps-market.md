<p><p>In-vehicle Op Amps come in three main types: single channel, dual channels, and quad channels. Single channel Op Amps have one input and one output, making them ideal for simple applications. Dual channel Op Amps have two separate Op Amps in one package, offering more flexibility for applications requiring multiple signals to be processed independently. Quad channel Op Amps have four separate Op Amps in one package, providing even more versatility for complex signal processing needs. Each type caters to different requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
