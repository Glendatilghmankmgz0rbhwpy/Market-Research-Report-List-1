<p><p>Hydraulic pumps for excavators are essential components that generate the necessary power to move fluids within the hydraulic system. Two common types of hydraulic pumps used in excavators are gear pumps and piston pumps. Gear pumps are simple and cost-effective, suitable for low to medium pressure applications. On the other hand, piston pumps are more complex and efficient, ideal for high-pressure operation in heavy-duty applications. Both gear and piston pumps play crucial roles in ensuring the proper functioning of hydraulic systems in excavators.</p></p>
