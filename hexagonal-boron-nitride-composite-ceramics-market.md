<p><p>Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics can be classified into two main types based on their structure: Graphite Structure Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics and Diamond Structure Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics. The former type exhibits properties similar to graphite, such as good lubricity and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for applications like lubricants and thermal management materials. On the other hand, Diamond Structure Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics have properties comparable to diamond, including high hardness and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for cutting tools and heat sinks.</p></p>
