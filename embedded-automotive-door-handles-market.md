<p><strong>The Embedded Automotive Door Handles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Button Type</li><li>Induction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Button Type embedded automotive door handles require the user to press a physical button to unlock the door. This type of handle is simple and reliable. On the other hand, Induction Type handles use sensors to detect the presence of the key fob or the user's hand near the handle, automatically unlocking the door. This type provides a touchless and convenient experience for the user. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the automotive market.</p></p>
