<p><p>Non-network connected non-IC card power smart meters come in two main market types: Simplex and Three Phase. They can accurately measure and monitor electricity consumption without the need for a network connection or IC card. Three-phase smart meters, on the other hand, are suitable for larger commercial or industrial installations with three-phase power supply.
