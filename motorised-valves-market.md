<p><p>Motorised valves market includes various types such as ball valves, butterfly valves, and knife gate valves. Ball valves are spherical in shape and provide tight shut-off, suitable for various industrial applications. Butterfly valves have a disc that rotates to control flow, ideal for large pipelines. Knife gate valves have a sharp edge that cuts through thick slurry or viscous fluids, commonly used in wastewater treatment plants. Each type caters to different requirements and industries, offering efficient and reliable flow control solutions.</p></p>
