<p><p>Thermoset carbon fiber prepreg market is categorized into various types based on the type of resin used. Epoxy resin is widely used due to its excellent mechanical properties and good adhesion to carbon fibers. Phenolic resin is known for its high heat resistance and fire retardant properties. Cyanate ester resin offers high temperature stability and low outgassing characteristics. Other types of resins used in thermoset carbon fiber prepreg include polyester resin, vinyl ester resin, and polyurethane resin, each offering specific properties for different applications.</p></p>
