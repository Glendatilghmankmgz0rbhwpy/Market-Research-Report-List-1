<p><p>Automotive refrigeration equipment market can be categorized into Single Temperature Type and Multi-Temperature Type. Single Temperature Type equipment is designed to cool products to a single set temperature, suitable for transporting goods that require consistent temperature control. On the other hand, Multi-Temperature Type equipment allows for different temperature zones within the same storage area, accommodating a variety of products with different cooling requirements in a single unit. This flexibility makes Multi-Temperature Type equipment more versatile for transporting diverse cargo.</p></p>
