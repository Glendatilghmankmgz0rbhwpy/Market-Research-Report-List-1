<p><p>Endoscope valves are essential components in endoscopy procedures, helping to regulate the flow of fluids and gases during examinations. These valves come in two main types - disposable and reusable. Disposable valves are meant for single-use only and are discarded after each procedure, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of infections. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and safety of endoscopic procedures.</p></p>
