<p><p>Periodically Poled Lithium Niobate Chips come in three main market types: Single Grating, Multi-grating, and Fanout Grating. Single Grating chips contain a single set of gratings, allowing for specific wavelength conversion. Multi-grating chips have multiple sets of gratings, enabling a wider range of wavelength conversion options. Fanout Grating chips have a fanout structure, providing enhanced performance and flexibility in wavelength conversion applications. Each market type caters to different needs and requirements in various industries such as telecommunications, spectroscopy, and photonics.</p></p>
