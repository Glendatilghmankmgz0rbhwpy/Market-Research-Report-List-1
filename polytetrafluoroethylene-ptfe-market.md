The market for PTFE can be categorized into two types - Particles PTFE and Fine Powder PTFE. Particles PTFE is used in applications where larger particle sizes are required, such as in the production of tapes and gaskets. Fine Powder PTFE, on the other hand, is used in applications where a finer particle size is needed, such as in the production of coatings and lubricants.</p></p>
