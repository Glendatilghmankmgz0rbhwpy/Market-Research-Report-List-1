<p><p>Water vapor barrier films for OLED screens are essential for protecting the sensitive organic materials from moisture damage. There are three main types of barrier films: organic, inorganic, and composite. Organic barrier films rely on organic materials like polymers to create a protective layer. Inorganic barrier films use inorganic materials such as metals or ceramics for barrier properties. Composite barrier films combine both organic and inorganic materials to provide a higher level of protection against water vapor transmission. Each type has its own benefits and applications in the OLED market.</p></p>
