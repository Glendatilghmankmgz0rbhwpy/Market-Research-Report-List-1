<p><p>Synthetic zeolite adsorbents are categorized into two types: regenerative and non-regenerative. Regenerative zeolite adsorbents can be reused multiple times by undergoing a regeneration process, making them cost-effective and sustainable. On the other hand, non-regenerative zeolite adsorbents are used for single-time applications as they cannot be regenerated. Both types of zeolite adsorbents are used in various industries such as gas separation, water treatment, and chemical processing for their ability to selectively adsorb molecules based on size and polarity.</p></p>
